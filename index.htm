What is the purpose of perfume?

A nice fragrance adds to your overall impression of being a sharp, put-together man.
We know that dressing well ups your confidence. You just feel better when you look nice.
Wearing a cologne enhances that feeling, as you'll be confident that you not only
look like a million bucks, but smell like it too.

Why is perfume used?

Perfume is used to give a pleasant and desirable scent to a person's body,
typically with the aim of increasing self-appeal and self-confidence.
